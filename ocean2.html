Marine Life & Conservation Efforts

Marine Life

The ocean is home to millions of species, from microscopic plankton to the largest animals on Earth, such as blue whales. Marine life also includes coral reefs, fish, sea turtles, and sharks, all of which play a vital role in the ocean ecosystem.

Ocean Conservation

The oceans face numerous threats, including plastic pollution, overfishing, and climate change. Efforts to conserve marine life include marine protected areas, sustainable fishing practices, and cleaning up ocean waste.
